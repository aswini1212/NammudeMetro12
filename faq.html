Nammude Metro - Frequently Asked Questions

Nammude Metro operates from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM every day, including weekends and public holidays.

You can purchase tickets through the Nammude Metro mobile app, from ticket counters at stations, or through automated ticket vending machines.

Yes, Nammude Metro offers discounted fares for students, senior citizens, and differently-abled passengers. You can avail these discounts by showing a valid ID at the ticket counter.

Most Nammude Metro stations have dedicated parking facilities. However, parking availability may vary depending on the station.
